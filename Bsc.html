BSc in Mathematics

The BSc in Mathematics program provides students with a strong foundation in mathematical theories and practices. It includes core modules that cover various fields in mathematics, from calculus and linear algebra to statistics and mathematical modeling. Graduates are well-prepared to pursue careers in research, education, finance, and data analysis.

Year I - Modules ▾
  • Calculus I
  • Linear Algebra
  • Introduction to Mathematical Reasoning
  • Probability and Statistics I
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Academic Writing
Year II - Modules ▾
  • Calculus II
  • Abstract Algebra
  • Numerical Methods
  • Probability and Statistics II
  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Elective I
Year III - Modules ▾
  • Real Analysis
  • Differential Equations
  • Complex Analysis
  • Linear Optimization
  • Computational Mathematics
  • Elective II
Year IV - Modules ▾
  • Advanced Calculus
  • Topology
  • Research Project
  • Mathematics of Finance
  • Elective III
  • Capstone Project
