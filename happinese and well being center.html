About Us

Welcome to Sherubtse College's Happiness and Well-Being Center. Our goal is to create a nurturing environment for personal growth, development, and psychological well-being.

Vision

To promote an on-campus culture of wellness that ensures happiness, psychological well-being, and lifelong success.

Mission

To create and provide an environment that fosters students’ growth, development, and psychological well-being through counseling services and awareness programs.

Background Information

The Happiness and Well-Being Center at Sherubtse College is dedicated to supporting students and staff through professional counseling services and awareness initiatives. Established in February 2016, the center is located within the Student Service Village and is run by a team of well-trained co-counselors.

Our services are aimed at enhancing personal and psychological well-being, empowering individuals to achieve happiness and success.

Services

Happiness & Well-Being Center

The Sherubtse Happiness and Well-being Center (HWC) is here to help you fulfill your academic, social, emotional, and personal potential by gaining understanding and insight into your strengths and challenges, and to help you put into effect changes that will enable you to achieve your desired result.

Services Offered

  • One to one counselling
  • Group counselling
  • Mindfulness and Yoga
  • Information on digital platform
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Self-awareness and self-leadership
  • Mindfulness-Based Basic Counselling Skills
  • Stress and Time Management

Location

Inside the Student Service Village, top floor, above the office of DSA, rear to cafeteria road.

Digital Platform

The center has a virtual digital platform that serves as a communication tool, resource bank, and information center for students and staff of Sherubtse. The platform has different features dedicated to several services, including online counseling services.

Student Counselling
