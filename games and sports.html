Games & Sports at Sherubtse College

At Sherubtse College, we believe that Games and Sports are an integral aspect of mental, emotional, and physical well-being. They teach important lessons about teamwork, tenacity, and resilience. The College encourages both students and staff to actively participate in various sports activities, fostering an environment of health, wellness, and camaraderie.

The College hosts various intra-college tournaments, such as departmental competitions, class competitions, hostel competitions, and an annual marathon. Additionally, Sherubtse College takes part in regional, national, and international tournaments organized by the Bhutan University Sports Federation (BUSF).

Sports Facilities

The College is equipped with top-notch sports facilities that cater to a wide range of games and sports. These facilities are available for both practice and competitions and are accessible to all members of the Sherubtse community.

Football Ground

The College has an international-sized football ground with astro turf. This facility is used not only for football matches but also for the celebration of major events, providing a versatile space for both sports and cultural activities.

Basketball Courts

Sherubtse College boasts two basketball courts: one located near the clock tower and the other on campus II. These courts are available for practice and matches, except on Mondays (maintenance day).

Volleyball Court

A volleyball court is located near the College football field, offering a space for practice and matches.

Tennis Court

The College features a well-maintained tennis court available to students and staff.

Gymnasium (GYM)

The College also has an indoor gymnasium located inside the multipurpose hall. It is equipped with basic facilities for bodybuilding and other exercises. A student trainer, supervised by the College Coach, manages the gym.

Archery Range

An archery range is available for practice and tournaments, contributing to the development of students' focus and precision.

Multipurpose Hall

The Multipurpose Hall houses three badminton courts and two table tennis tables. This indoor space provides ample opportunities for various indoor sports activities.

Futsal Ground

A futsal ground is also available for students to engage in this fast-paced form of football.
