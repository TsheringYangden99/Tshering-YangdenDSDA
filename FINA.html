Forum for International & National Awareness (FINA)

Background Information

The Forum for International and National Awareness (FINA) was formally formed on 28th May 1989 as a result of the merger of the earlier societies. The idea of creating such a Forum was initiated by the principal, Dasho Zanglay Drukpa, in the interest of common and a stronger forum.

With a huge enrolment of students every year, the role of FINA is expected to ease the management in administrating the college. It is to consider the issues and to review, recommend, or formulate policies in areas or exclusively involving the student body. The FINA also seeks to provide extended channels of communication, increased areas of cooperation, and greater acceptance of shared responsibility between the faculty, administration, and student body in matters of general and academic affairs.

Mission

To enhance communication, cooperation, and shared responsibility among faculty, administration, and students in general and academic affairs.

Vision

Inspiring Excellence, Fostering Unity, Empowering Tomorrow, Unleashing Potential, and Igniting Minds to not just produce successful and professional citizens but active contributors to the well-being of society.
