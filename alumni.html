Sherubtse Alumni

Sherubtse Alumni Association welcomes all alumni: staff, faculty, students, and graduates of Sherubtse College to maintain a mutually beneficial and lifelong relationship among Sherubtse alumni and Sherubtse College through the Sherubtse Alumni Association. Your connection with Sherubtse College does not end when you graduate, stop working, or resign from the College. The Sherubtse Alumni Association ensures that you stay connected through alumni matters so that you can remain a part of the Sherubtse Family forever. The College would like to register all of you with the Sherubtse Alumni Association and be part of the Sherubtse family, fostering a lifelong relationship and connection
