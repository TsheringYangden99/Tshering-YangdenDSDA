BSc in Life Science

The BSc in Life Science program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of biological sciences. It covers essential topics in biology, from molecular and cellular biology to ecology and environmental science. Graduates will be equipped to contribute to advancements in health, biotechnology, conservation, and environmental sustainability.
