Sherubtse Timeline

1
1968

Sherubtse is Born

Sherubtse High School was established, inaugurated by the Third King Jigme Dorji Wangchuck.

2
1976

Junior College Started

Junior College started with 23 students enrolled.

3
1983

Undergraduate Courses Started

Bachelor's Degree courses started with 8 students in B.A, B.Com., and B.Sc programs.

4
2004

Joined RUB

Sherubtse College joins other colleges to establish the Royal University of Bhutan.

5
2006

Started Degree Programmes

Sherubtse started offering degree programmes under the Royal University of Bhutan in Arts, Science, and Commerce.

6
2008

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Math and Physics, BSc in Math and Chemistry, and BSc in Physics and Chemistry were introduced.

7
2009

Double Subject Degree Programmes

Introduced BA in Political Science and Sociology, BA in History and Dzongkha, and BA in Dzongkha and English.

8
2011

Established a New Research Centre

Centre for Climate Change and Spatial Information (CCCSI) established.

9
2014

Introduced Two New Research Centres

Centre for Archaeology and Historical Research (CAHR) and Centre for Science and Environmental Research (CSER) introduced.

10
2017

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and BA in Population and Development Studies introduced.

11
2019

Introduced Single Degree Programmes

BA in Economics, English, Media Studies, History, and BSc in Geography introduced.

12
2021

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Data Science and Statistics programmes introduced in the Department of Mathematical Sciences.
