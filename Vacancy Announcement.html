Vacancy Announcement - Site Supervising Engineer

Sherubtse College is pleased to announce the following vacancy for the position of Site Supervising Engineer for immediate recruitment.

Sl. No Position Title Position Level Qualification Slot Mode of Employment
1 Site Supervising Engineer 7 Minimum of Bachelors in Civil Engineering with two years of experience in relevant field 1 Contract
Application Details

Eligible candidates may submit an application via the online portal RUB IMS. Applicants are required to register as job applicants via https://ims.rub.edu.bt and then apply by the deadline of 18th November, 2024, 4:00 PM.

Position Profile

Site Supervising Engineer

Eligibility Criteria
  • Must be a Bhutanese citizen.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age and not more than 45 years of age.
  • Meet qualification, experience, and other requirements as specified in the position profile.
Required Documents
  • Degree Certificate of Bachelor’s degree and above (if any).
  • Mark sheets of Bachelor’s degree and above (if any).
  • Mark sheets of Class XII and Class X.
  • Valid Security Clearance Certificate.
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate for Employment/Interview.
  • Valid Citizenship ID card (both sides).
  • Valid Audit Clearance Certificate (Interview), if employed.
  • Experience Certificates (if applicable).
Selection Process

Shortlisted candidates will be announced on the College website and intimated for an interview through a call.

Important Notes

The two references in the application form should be professional referees. If employed, one referee should be from the current or previous organization, and the other should be from the school/college/university where the highest qualification was obtained. Incomplete applications will be rejected during the initial scrutiny.

Contact Information

For further information, please contact the HR Division at 04-535288 during office hours.
