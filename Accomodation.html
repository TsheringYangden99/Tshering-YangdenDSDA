Accommodation

New Male and Female Residence Hall Allocation 2024

Welcome to the 2024 academic year! We are excited to announce the residence hall allocations for new students, with dedicated halls for both male and female students, ensuring a comfortable and inclusive living environment for everyone.

On-campus Accommodation

Sherubtse has 20 on-campus residential accommodations (10 female residences and 10 male residences) with free unlimited Wi-Fi facilities. Students admitted on Government scholarship receive a monthly stipend of Nu. 2,500, of which Nu. 250 is deducted every month towards rent for accommodation.

Centrally Catered Residence Halls

There are three centrally catered residence halls for females and two for males. Meals are provided from the common mess. All Year 1 students are allocated centrally catered residence halls during their first year. Students on Government scholarships residing in centrally catered residence halls are not paid a stipend since Nu. 2250 is used for providing meals and other miscellaneous expenditures, with Nu. 250 deducted as rent. Self-financed students in these residence halls pay Nu. 2250 per month for mess expenses.

Self-catering Residence Halls

All students after completing Year 1 are allocated self-catering residence halls. There are sixteen self-catering residence halls: 8 for male students and 8 for female students. Students manage their meals independently and receive a stipend of Nu. 2250 after deducting Nu. 250 as rent.
